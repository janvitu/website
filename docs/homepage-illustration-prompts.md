## 1. Lone Mountain Peak

```
Create a sumi-e Japanese ink wash painting of a single mountain peak emerging from mist.
Style: traditional East Asian ink wash (sumi-e), monochromatic using only black, dark gray, and light gray ink tones. Background must be pure solid white (#ffffff) — no paper texture, no grain, no aged or cream tones, no watercolor paper effect. Flat digital white only.
Composition: horizontal orientation. The peak occupies the upper half of the canvas, sharp and confident with dark bold brushstrokes at the summit. The mountain body narrows quickly, dissolving into soft horizontal fog below the midpoint. The lower half is almost entirely white with only the faintest suggestion of fog as diffused gray wash. The peak feels isolated, exact, inevitable.
Texture: dry brush at the summit edge showing jagged rock texture, wet ink wash bleeds on the upper slopes, the fog below is a single soft wash with no defined lower boundary.
No paths, no trees, no water, no additional elements. Just the peak and the mist it rises from.
IMPORTANT: the entire illustration must be fully contained within the canvas. Leave generous white margin space on all four sides (top, bottom, left, right). No ink, no brushstrokes, no splatters, no shapes touching or bleeding past the edges of the canvas.
No text, no labels, no annotations.
Pure solid white background (#ffffff), no texture, no paper grain, no border, no frame.
```

## 3. Crane in Flight

```
Create a sumi-e Japanese ink wash painting of a single crane in flight.
Style: traditional East Asian ink wash (sumi-e), monochromatic using only black, dark gray, and light gray ink tones. Background must be pure solid white (#ffffff) — no paper texture, no grain, no aged or cream tones, no watercolor paper effect. Flat digital white only.
Composition: horizontal orientation. The crane is centered slightly above the midline, wings fully extended spanning most of the canvas width. The body and head are rendered in bold dark ink with confident brushstrokes, the wingtips fade into lighter gray at the edges. The crane faces left, moving with intention. Generous white space surrounds the bird on all sides — the emptiness is part of the composition.
Texture: bold dry brush on the body and primary feathers, wet ink wash bleed on the secondary wing feathers, fine ink splatters near the wingtips suggesting motion.
No landscape, no water, no reeds, no additional elements. Just the crane and the white space it moves through.
IMPORTANT: the entire illustration must be fully contained within the canvas. Leave generous white margin space on all four sides (top, bottom, left, right). No ink, no brushstrokes, no splatters, no shapes touching or bleeding past the edges of the canvas.
No text, no labels, no annotations.
Pure solid white background (#ffffff), no texture, no paper grain, no border, no frame.
```

## Usage Notes

- Export as AVIF with white background
- White background disappears on the site via `mix-blend-multiply` CSS
- Horizontal orientation — homepage hero use, wider than tall
- Monochromatic only — accent color (#ff4309) is added via the site, not the illustrations
